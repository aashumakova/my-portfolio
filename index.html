Learn your bones

Learn your bones is a guessing game for premed/nursing students who are interested in learning the spelling of 206 bones of the human body. The game was developed with Vanilla Javascript, CSS3, and HTML5 to organize and display the visual aspects of the application. A randomized word is generated at every stage. Object oriented functions were created to iterate over letters in the word as users start guessing the mystery word.
